SWIMMING DURING PERIODS

Is it okay:
Yes, many girls can swim during their period.

What to use:
Some people use a tampon or menstrual cup for swimming. Others choose not to swim during a heavy flow.

Helpful tip:
Change into dry clothes afterward and use a fresh pad once you are out of the water if needed.

When to seek help:
If you feel dizzy, have severe pain, or feel very unwell, stop and tell a trusted adult.
