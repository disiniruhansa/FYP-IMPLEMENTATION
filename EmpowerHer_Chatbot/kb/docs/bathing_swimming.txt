BATHING AND SWIMMING DURING PERIODS

Is it safe:
Yes, bathing during your period is safe and helps you feel clean and comfortable.

Swimming:
Some people choose to swim using appropriate menstrual products. Others prefer to avoid swimming during heavy flow.

What to remember:
Change pads after bathing and keep yourself dry.

When to seek help:
If you feel pain, dizziness, or discomfort during bathing, talk to an adult or doctor.
