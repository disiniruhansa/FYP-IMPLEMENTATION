SPOTTING AND BROWN DISCHARGE

What it is:
Spotting means light bleeding between periods. Brown discharge is often old blood leaving the body.

What is normal:
Light spotting or brown discharge can happen at the start or end of a period, or due to hormone changes.

Possible causes:
Stress, irregular cycles, or changes in routine can cause spotting.

When to be careful:
If spotting happens often, lasts many days, or comes with pain, bad smell, or itching.

When to seek help:
If spotting continues regularly, becomes heavy, or causes discomfort, talk to a doctor or clinic.
