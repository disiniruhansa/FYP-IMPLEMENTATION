PMS AND MOOD SWINGS

What it is:
Hormone changes before periods can affect mood, causing sadness, anger, or irritation.

What is normal:
Feeling more emotional before a period is common.

What may help:
Rest, calming music, talking to someone you trust, and light activity.

When to seek help:
If mood changes feel overwhelming, very dark, or unsafe, talk to a counsellor or doctor.
