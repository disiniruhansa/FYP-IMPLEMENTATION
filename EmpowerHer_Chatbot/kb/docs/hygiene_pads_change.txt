MENSTRUAL HYGIENE AND PAD CHANGING

Why hygiene matters:
Good hygiene helps prevent irritation, infection, and discomfort during periods.

Pad changing:
Many people change pads every 4–6 hours, depending on flow. Change sooner if the pad feels wet or uncomfortable.

What helps:
Wash with clean water, keep the area dry, and wear clean underwear.

When to seek help:
If you have itching, burning, rashes, or strong odor, talk to a clinic or doctor.
