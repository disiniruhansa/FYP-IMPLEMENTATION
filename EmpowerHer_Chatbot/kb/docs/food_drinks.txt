

Ice cream and cold foods:
Eating ice cream is usually safe during periods. Cold foods do not stop periods.

What to notice:
Some people feel cold foods make cramps worse. This depends on the individual.

Helpful foods:
Balanced meals, fruits, vegetables, and enough water can help energy and mood.

When to seek help:
If you feel weak, dizzy, or unwell during periods, talk to a doctor or clinic.
