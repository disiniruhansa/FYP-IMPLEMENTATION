PERIOD SMELL AND HYGIENE

What is normal: A mild, earthy smell during periods can happen because blood and tissue are leaving the body. Clear or white discharge with little or no smell between periods is also normal.

Freshness tips: Change pads regularly, wash the vulva with water (no harsh soaps inside the vagina), and wear breathable cotton underwear. If you notice strong or fishy odor with itching, burning, fever, or unusual discharge, please talk to a trusted adult or visit a clinic.
