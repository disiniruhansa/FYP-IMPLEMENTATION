NORMAL VAGINAL DISCHARGE

What it is:
Discharge is a fluid that helps keep the vagina clean and healthy.

What is normal:
Clear or white discharge with little or no smell is normal. It may change during the cycle.

What is not normal:
Strong smell, green or yellow color, itching, burning, or pain.

What you can do:
Keep the area clean with water, wear clean underwear, and avoid harsh soaps.

When to seek help:
If discharge has a strong smell, causes itching or pain, or looks unusual, talk to a doctor or clinic.
