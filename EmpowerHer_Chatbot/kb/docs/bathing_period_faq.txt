BATHING AND SHOWERING ON YOUR PERIOD

Is it safe:
Yes, bathing or showering during your period is safe and can help you feel clean and more comfortable.

What to remember:
Wash gently with water, avoid harsh soaps inside the vagina, and change to a clean pad or menstrual product afterward if needed.

What is not true:
Bathing does not stop your period or trap the blood inside your body.

When to seek help:
If you feel faint, weak, or have severe pain, tell a trusted adult.
