PERIOD CRAMPS RELIEF (ALTERNATIVE TIPS)

What it is: Period cramps happen when the uterus tightens to push out the lining. They can show up in the lower belly or lower back.

Self-care steps: Sip warm water or ginger tea, use a warm pad on your belly or back, stretch gently, and try light walks to loosen muscles. Eating balanced meals and keeping caffeine low can also help some people.

When to get help: If you have severe pain that makes it hard to move, heavy bleeding, fever, fainting, or dizziness, please talk to a trusted adult or visit a clinic.
