SIGNS BEFORE A FIRST PERIOD

Common signs:
White or clear discharge, breast development, body hair growth, mild cramps, and mood changes can happen before a first period starts.

What is normal:
The first period often starts between ages 9 and 15, but timing can vary.

How to prepare:
Keep pads ready, wear underwear you feel comfortable in, and talk to a trusted adult or school nurse if you have questions.

When to seek help:
If you are worried or confused, it is okay to ask a trusted adult, nurse, or doctor.
