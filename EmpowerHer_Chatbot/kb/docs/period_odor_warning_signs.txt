STRONG PERIOD ODOR

What can be normal:
A mild earthy smell during a period can happen.

What is more concerning:
A strong or fishy smell, especially with itching, burning, unusual discharge, or fever, can mean you should get checked.

Freshness tips:
Change pads regularly, wash the outside area gently with water, and wear breathable underwear.

When to seek help:
If the smell is strong, keeps happening, or comes with pain or fever, tell a trusted adult and visit a clinic.
