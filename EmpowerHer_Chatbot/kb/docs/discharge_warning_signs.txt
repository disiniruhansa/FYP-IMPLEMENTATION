DISCHARGE: WHAT IS NORMAL AND WHAT IS NOT

Usually normal:
Clear or white discharge with little or no smell is common and helps keep the vagina healthy.

Warning signs:
Green or yellow discharge, a strong fishy smell, itching, burning, pain, or fever are not things to ignore.

What may help:
Wear clean underwear, wash gently with water, and avoid harsh soaps or scented products.

When to seek help:
If discharge looks unusual or causes discomfort, talk to a trusted adult or visit a clinic.
